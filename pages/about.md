## GAIR Lab: Generative AI Research Laboratory

GAIR Lab is a research initiative established at BITS Pilani in collaboration with Skillplot, dedicated to advancing the frontiers of Generative AI and Agentic Intelligence. Our work bridges academic rigor with practical implementation, focusing on real-world applications and open-source contributions.

### Our Mission

To conduct cutting-edge research in Generative AI and develop practical, open-source tools that advance the field while mentoring the next generation of AI researchers and engineers.

### Our Vision

GAIR Lab envisions a future where:
- Agentic AI systems are accessible to researchers and practitioners worldwide
- AI research translates into tangible, deployable solutions
- Academic institutions and industry collaborate seamlessly on impactful research
- Open-source contributions accelerate innovation across the AI community

## Research Focus Areas

### 1. Agentic AI in Cybersecurity
Building autonomous AI agents that can understand and execute complex security operations, bridging the gap between AI and practical cybersecurity tooling.

### 2. Multimodal Intelligence
Developing advanced Vision-Language Models for understanding complex real-world scenarios, from surveillance analysis to scientific research.

### 3. Voice & Creative AI
Exploring emotion-aware voice synthesis, voice cloning, and AI-assisted creative content generation, particularly for underrepresented languages like Hindi.

### 4. Scientific AI (Bio-AI)
Leveraging AI agents for accelerating biological research, including protein structure prediction, drug discovery workflows, and scientific data exploration.

### Research Teams
GAIR Lab organizes research into project-based teams, each focusing on a specific technical domain:
- Project Kali: Agentic Cybersecurity
- Project Vaani: Audio & Creative AI
- Project Drishti: Multimodal Intelligence
- Project Jigyasa: Scientific AI & Bio-Computing

Each project operates with dedicated student teams, mentorship, and clear research objectives.
