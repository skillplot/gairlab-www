## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When can students apply for internships?**  
A: We recruit year-round, but primary internship periods are summer and winter breaks. Check [Projects]({{ '/projects/' | relative_url }}) for current openings.

**Q: What are the prerequisites for joining GAIR Lab?**  
A: Strong coding fundamentals and curiosity about AI. Specific prerequisites vary by projectâ€”see individual project pages.

**Q: Can I contribute remotely?**  
A: Yes! While our lab is based at BITS Pilani, we support remote collaboration for both students and external contributors.

**Q: Is there a fee to participate in research?**  
A: No. GAIR Lab is a research initiative. Internships are either credited or can include stipends depending on the project and semester.

**Q: How do I stay updated on GAIR Lab news?**  
A: Join our WhatsApp community or [subscribe to updates]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) via this form.
